Our Story

From a Small Village in Sindh to Your Table

Sindhi Zaika Achaar is a home-based business run by Raza Ali Khaskheli from the village of Karam Khan Nizamani, District Matiari, Sindh.

For years, Raza Ali has been preparing traditional handmade Sindhi achaar using centuries-old family recipes. What started as local sharing quickly grew into demand from people across Sindh and beyond.

This online store is our way of bringing authentic Sindhi flavours to every corner of Pakistan — without compromising on the quality, taste, or tradition.
